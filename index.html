Welcome to ECC - Engineers Contracting Company

info@ecc-india.co.in

Foundation:    Established in 2004, ECC is one of the reputed contractors in Hyderabad which deliver social and economic residential development projects including high-rise developments, residential buildings, luxury villas and industrial warehouses. ECC has undertaken projects in various developing regions in Hyderabad.

For more than 14 years, ECC has built a reputation for quality and solid expertise in simple and complex constructions. Its ability to manage various projects efficiently and effectively has enabled the company to successfully complete more than 120 projects.

ECC's biggest asset is the highly skilled and dedicated team who adopt a truly collaborative approach to their work. They understand the complexities required for the seamless integration of different construction methodologies, ensuring the on-time and on-budget delivery of the projects.

ECC prides itself on being a contractor of choice, its reputation for quality, and its long standing relationships with clients, partners and shareholders
